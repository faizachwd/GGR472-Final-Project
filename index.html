CanFed Map

The Canadian Food Environment Dataset (Can-FED) analyzes the retail food environment in Canada. It includes data on 19 types of food outlets, such as grocery stores and fast-food restaurants, organized by dissemination areas (DA). Key metrics include the Modified Retail Food Environment Index (mRFEI) and Fast-Food Restaurant Mix (Rmix), which measure access to healthy food and the proportion of fast-food outlets, respectively.
